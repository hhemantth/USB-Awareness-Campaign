Think Before You Plug

You just ran a simulated ransomware payload from a USB device.

This was part of a security awareness campaign designed to highlight the risks of unknown USBs and encourage safe practices.

  • Never plug in unknown USB drives
  • Disable autorun features on your system
  • Report suspicious devices to your IT or security team
  • Use endpoint protection tools and USB control policies

Stay alert. Stay secure. Your awareness is your first line of defense.
